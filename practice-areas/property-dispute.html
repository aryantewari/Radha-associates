Property Practice

Property Dispute & Title Due Diligence

Safeguarding your real estate investments across Karnataka. Before you buy, sell or litigate — we verify the title is clear, compliant, and free from hidden disputes.

Verification Checklist

What We Examine

Every property carries a paper trail. We trace it end to end so you know exactly what you're signing up for.

CategoryDocuments & Checks
Ownership HistoryOriginal Mother Deed, 30-year Encumbrance Certificate (EC), and RTC (Form 11A / Pahani).
Municipal & ZoningBBMP / BDA Khata verification, land conversion (NA) orders, and up-to-date property tax receipts.
Construction LegalitySanctioned building plan, Occupancy Certificate (OC), and RERA registration status where applicable.
Litigation SearchKarnataka High Court, DRT, and local civil court records — checked for pending suits, injunctions, or attachments.
Financial EncumbrancesBank mortgages, loan liens, and any charges registered against the property.
How We Work

Our Process

Document Collection

Sale deeds, Khatas, tax receipts and government approvals gathered from you and public records.

Legal Scrutiny

Expert review of the encumbrance chain and title history for gaps or defects.

Litigation Search

High Court and local court records checked for any ongoing or past disputes.

Legal Opinion

A plain-language report summarising risk, and how to proceed or resolve a dispute.
