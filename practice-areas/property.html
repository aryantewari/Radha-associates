Securing Your Real Estate Investments

Real estate represents one of the most significant financial investments for individuals and corporations alike. Navigating property laws, zoning regulations, and title complexities requires meticulous legal scrutiny to prevent devastating future liabilities.

Adv. Radha Chaudhary brings extensive experience in handling high-stakes property disputes, conveyancing, and complex partitions. We ensure that your property transactions are legally sound, transparent, and free from encumbrances, whether you are buying your first home or developing a commercial high-rise.

Flawless Title Verification

We conduct deep due diligence to uncover hidden litigation, unregistered agreements, or title defects, ensuring your hard-earned money is invested in clear, marketable titles.

Our Property Law Expertise

Title Verification

Comprehensive background checks on property histories, flow of title, and encumbrance certificates to ensure clear, marketable properties free from litigation.

RERA Disputes

Aggressive representation for homebuyers and developers in the Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) regarding delayed possessions and project defaults.

Partition & Succession

Resolving complex family property partitions, ancestral wealth disputes, and drafting legally binding settlement and relinquishment deeds.

Conveyancing & Drafting

Drafting, vetting, and registering Sale Deeds, Lease Agreements, Joint Development Agreements (JDA), and General Powers of Attorney (GPA).

Landlord & Tenant Disputes

Handling evictions, rent recovery, lease breaches, and drafting watertight commercial and residential rental agreements to prevent future conflicts.

Land Acquisition & Zoning

Advising on land use conversions, agricultural to non-agricultural (DC Conversion), and representation for government acquisition compensations.
